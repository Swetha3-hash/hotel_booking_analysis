# 🏨 Hotel Booking Cancellation & Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem
Hotels face significant revenue loss due to high 
cancellation rates. This project identifies key 
cancellation drivers and provides actionable 
business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Numpy)
- MySQL Workbench
- Power BI

## Key Findings
- Overall cancellation rate: 37%
- City Hotel cancels at 42% vs Resort Hotel 28%
- Bookings made 90+ days in advance cancel at 58%
- Estimated £12.9M revenue gap annually

## Recommendations
4 data-driven recommendations to recover 
20-30% of lost revenue annually:
1. Lead Time Pricing Strategy
2. City Hotel Deposit Policy
3. Online TA Channel Strategy
4. Seasonal Revenue Optimisation
